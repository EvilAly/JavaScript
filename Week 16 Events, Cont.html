Part 1

Your challenge is to make the square box below fill with a green overlay when you hover over it, but disappear again when you move your mouse away.

  1. select the box element, saving it to a variable
  2. on the mouseover event, add the class effect-active, but do not modify or remove any other classes or properties of this element.
  3. on the mouseout event, remove the class effect-active, again not modifying or removing any other classes or properties of this element.
